El Imposible National Park

Immerse yourself in this Wonderful Forest

El Imposible is a rainforest, in parts tropical and in others sub-tropical. Located on the Sierra de Apaneca-Ilamatepeque, it has the greatest biodiversity in the country as part of a mature forest, typical of early Mesoamerica.

El Imposible National Park, open to the public since 1997, offers tourists: camping areas, an interpretation center, interpretive and conventional trails, viewpoints, community guides, as well as adequate infrastructure to accommodate visitors.

For more information about this destination, you can visit the Ministry of Environment and Natural Resources website https://marn.gob.sv/servicios/ingresoanp/.

For more information, cabin reservations and permits, you can write to visitasanp@marn.gob.sv or mmarcia@marn.gob.sv.
