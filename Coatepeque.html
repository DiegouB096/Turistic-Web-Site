Lake Coatepeque

Amazing and Mysterious Lake

Its blue waters and its high ecotourism offer make Lake Coatepeque one of the favorite destinations for Salvadorans and foreigners, here you can practice kayaking, jet skiing and diving.

This lake is of volcanic origin and due to its abundant natural wealth you can observe a great variety of resident and migratory birds, fish, dive or take a kayak, jet ski or boat ride. In addition, in the surroundings you could go hiking or mountain biking, achieving beautiful panoramic views full of vegetation.

Its waters change from blue to turquoise green, and according to authorities of the Ministry of Environment and Natural Resources of El Salvador this is a cyclical phenomenon that occurs from time to time. The color change has happened in 1998, 2006, 2012, 2016, 2017, 2018 and 2019.
