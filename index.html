Turistic Sites

Places you can visit in the same day or even spend days. Most of them are no more than 45 minutes away from the Capital.

Tamanique Waterfalls

Located just 20 minutes from the coast of La Libertad is the town of Tamanique. It is a place of cliffs and mountains and where you can combine the beach experience just minutes away.

Explore Now

Lake Coatepeque

Lake Coatepeque is of volcanic origin and is located 18 km south of the city of Santa Ana. It has an altitude of 745 meters above sea level and an area of 25.3 square kilometers. In addition, its depth of 115 m.

Explore Now

El Imposible National Park

El Imposible is a rainforest, in parts tropical and in others sub-tropical. Located on the Sierra de Apaneca-Ilamatepeque, it has the greatest biodiversity in the country as part of a mature forest, typical of early Mesoamerica.

Explore Now

El Tunco Beach

El Tunco is a black sand beach, it is located in the department of La Libertad. On this beach you can perform various activities, from sunbathing in a comfortable hammock, hiking, tasting its cuisine, to dominate the rapturous waves.

Explore Now

San Andres Archaeological Site

San Andrés is a pre-Hispanic Mayan site in El Salvador, whose long occupation began around 900 B.C. as an agricultural village in the Zapotitán valley of the department of La Libertad.

Explore Now

Santa Ana Volcano

Pon a prueba tu resistencia alcanzando la cima del volcán Ilamatepec, también llamado volcán de Santa Ana. Si lo logras ten por seguro que tendrás algunas de las vistas más impresionantes de la zona, teniendo de fondo las aguas turquesas del lago de Coatepeque.

Explore Now
